Interview Tips

The following provides basic guidelines on how to approach an upcoming interview. Especially tailored towards co-op job interviews, the main 12 points are listed below in the step by step manner in which they should be followed.


  1. Research the company and know it well
  2. Practice common interview questions - Glassdoor
  3. Prepare solid questions for interviewer - on the company, their experience, a challenging aspect
  4. Carry professional portfolio with copies of updated resume, references, opt: recommendations
  5. Always dress your best in suit and heels/suit and tie
  6. Be on time for the interview - scope out company location before your interview date
  7. Maintain confident body language - strong hand shakes, eye contact, sit upright
  8. Answer only the questions asked, and be mindful of using appropriate examples
  9. Always maintain professionalism in everything
  10. Make a good first impression and be memorable
  11. Ask for interviewer’s business card to follow-up later
  12. Send a thank you email to all interviews
